# Cyber-Warfare Attribution & Response

This skill acts as the system's 'Offensive Shield', identifying the source of any attack and deploying automated defensive/counter-narrative measures.

## Instructions
1. Trace attack signatures back to specific APT (Advanced Persistent Threat) groups.
2. Deploy 'Deception Grids' (Honey-pots) to collect data on the attacker's tools.
3. Automatically notify relevant global security bodies and draft press releases for 'Brand Defense'.
4. Trigger 'Encryption Hardening' protocols across the entire Sovereign Mesh if a state-level threat is confirmed.

## Examples
- "Identify the source of the recent DDoS attack on our Vercel frontend and propose a counter-move."
