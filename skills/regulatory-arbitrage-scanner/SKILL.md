# Regulatory Arbitrage Scanner

This skill focuses on 'Legal Optimization', scanning global regulations to find where a new feature or product can launch with minimum friction.

## Instructions
1. Maintain a database of regulations for 50+ countries (Crypto, AI, Privacy).
2. Compare tax rates, license requirements, and reporting duties.
3. Automatically recommend a 'Path of Least Resistance' for any new business objective.
4. Alert the user to 'Regulatory Havens' that emerge in real-time.

## Examples
- "Find the best country to register our new 8K animation studio to avoid complex copyright fees."
